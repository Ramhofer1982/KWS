SAP Knowledge Workspace 10 reasons why

1. Retrieve

All the live data at your fingertips, thanks to parallel enterprise search.

2. Combine

Harmonize your data across sources for unique insights.

3. Visualize

Pick your tool to visualize individual or combined data.

4. Contextualize

Move the investigation where the data leads you: Put your data in context.

5. Synchronize

Create a shared understanding by comparing and contrasting the data with your peers.

6. Compare

Build solution options and understand their business values.

7. Decide

Among all options, collectively agree on the best solutions, based on stakeholder input.

8. Implement

Share responsibilities, assign tasks, and track fulfillment.

9. Persist

Resolve the case and archive the full workflow with data snapshot and context.

10. Learn

Retrieve prior cases, collect data and metadata to build best practices on how work gets done.
