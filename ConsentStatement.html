A.      GENERAL INFORMATION

 

Data Controller. Data controller in case of Knowledge Workspace (KWS) is SAP SE, Dietmar-Hopp-Allee 16, 69190 Walldorf (SAP) with its data protection officer Mathias Cellarius (privacy@sap.com).

 

What does SAP do with my Personal Data? SAP will process or use any information that can be used to identify you (i.e. your Personal Data) only as set out in this Privacy Statement and either based on a statutory permission (see B. below) or your consent (see C. below).

 

Duration of processing of Personal Data. SAP will only use your Personal Data for as long as it is required to fulfil the purposes set out below or unless you withdraw your consent granted hereunder (if SAPs use of your Personal Data is based on your consent).

 

Why am I required to provide Personal Data? As a general principle, granting of any consent and the provision of any Personal Data is entirely voluntarily to you and there are no adverse consequences to you if you do not grant a consent or provide Personal Data. However, since the consent requested hereunder is a statutory requirement for SAP offering KWS to you, you cannot use KWS without providing SAP this consent.

 

Where will my Personal Data be processed? As part of a global group of companies, SAP has affiliates and third party service providers within as well as outside of the European Economic Area (EEA). As a consequence, whenever SAP is using or otherwise processing your Personal Data for the purposes set out in this Privacy Statement, SAP may transfer your Personal Data to countries outside of the European Economic Area including to such countries where a statutory level of data protection applies that is not comparable to the level of data protection within the EEA. Whenever such transfer occurs, it is based on the Standard Contractual Clauses in order to contractually ensure that your Personal Data is subject to a level of data protection which applies within the EEA. You may obtain a redacted copy (in order to remove commercial and not relevant information) of such Standard Contractual Clauses by sending a request to privacy@sap.com.

 

Data subjects rights. You can request from SAP at any time information about which Personal Data SAP has about you and the correction or deletion of such Personal Data (whereby SAP can only delete your Personal Data if there is no statutory contradicting obligation to retain it). Kindly note that if you request the deletion of your Personal Data from SAP you will not be able to further use the SAP service provided hereunder.

 

Furthermore, if SAPs use of your Personal Data is based on your consent or is required to perform a contract with you, beyond mere information about your Personal Data, you may request from SAP a copy of the provided Personal Data. In this case, please contact the email address below and state which Personal Data you require from SAP in which format and whether the Personal Data shall be provided to you or another recipient. SAP will carefully review your request and discuss with you how it can be best implemented.

 

Finally, you can request from SAP that SAP restricts your Personal Data from any further processing in any of the following events: (i) you state that the Personal Data SAP has about you is incorrect, however, only for as long as SAP requires to check the accuracy of the relevant Personal Data, (ii) there is no legal basis for SAP processing your Personal Data and you demand that SAP blocks your Personal Data from further processing or (iii) SAP does not longer require your Personal Data but you claim that you require such data in order to claim or exercise legal rights or to defend against third party claims.

 

Please direct any such request to SAP_KWS_Info@sap.com.

 

Right to lodge a complaint. If you take the view that SAP is not processing your Personal Data in accordance with the requirements set out herein or applicable EEA data protection laws, you can at any time lodge a complaint with the data protection authority of the EEA country where you live or with the data protection authority of the country or state where SAP has its registered seat.

 

Use of this website by children. This website is not directed to users of an age of below 16 years. If you are younger than 16, you may not register with and use this website.

 

Links to other websites. This website may contain links to foreign (i.e. non SAP group of companies) websites. SAP is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of other websites outside the SAP Group of companies. Therefore, we recommend that you carefully read the privacy statements of such foreign sites.
